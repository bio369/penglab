I am Liang Dong, currently an associate research fellow in Peng lab of GIBH. I have got PhD degree with a background in biomedical research from Shanghai institute of biochemistry and cell biology (SIBCB, CAS). After gaining PhD degree, I worked as post-doctor in Sun Yat-sen University and City University of Hong Kong respectively. I have been engaged in research on the mechanism of cell lineage differentiation in the process of tissue repair after injury.
